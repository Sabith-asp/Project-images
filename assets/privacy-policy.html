Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Access

  • Your LinkedIn profile name and email address — used only to identify your connected account within the App.
  • An OAuth access token — stored securely and used solely to perform actions you explicitly authorise.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information accessed through LinkedIn is used only to provide the core functionality of the App on your behalf. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what you directly request.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, or cookies.
  • We do not store your LinkedIn password or login credentials.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely and is not transmitted to any external server beyond the services you directly interact with (such as LinkedIn).

5. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services at your direction:

  • LinkedIn API — to perform actions on your LinkedIn account. See LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by revoking access through your LinkedIn account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To revoke the App's access to your LinkedIn account, visit your LinkedIn Permitted Services page and remove the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at github.com/Sabith-asp.
