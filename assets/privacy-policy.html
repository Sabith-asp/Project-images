Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the LinkedIn Automation Tool ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you authorise it to access your LinkedIn account.

1. What This App Does

The App is a self-hosted, personal-use tool that uses the LinkedIn API to publish content on your behalf. It is not a commercial service and is not offered to third parties.

2. Data We Access

  • Your LinkedIn profile name and email address — used only to display your connection status inside the App.
  • An OAuth access token — stored locally on your own machine/server and used solely to post content you explicitly approve.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not send your data to any third-party server.
  • We do not store, sell, or share your LinkedIn credentials or personal information with anyone.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies.

4. Data Storage

All data (OAuth tokens, post content, settings) is stored exclusively in a PostgreSQL database running on your own machine or private server. No data leaves your environment except for API calls made directly to LinkedIn and Anthropic (Claude AI) as initiated by you.

5. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following services at your direction:

  • LinkedIn API — to publish posts you have approved.
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate post content from your ideas.

Please refer to LinkedIn's Privacy Policy and Anthropic's Privacy Policy for how those services handle data.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as you keep it in your local database. You can delete any data at any time by removing records from the database or uninstalling the App entirely.

7. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally under your control, you have full access to view, modify, and delete it at any time. To revoke LinkedIn access, disconnect the App from the Settings page or visit your LinkedIn Permitted Services page.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

This is a personal, self-hosted tool. For questions, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
